### Small Accounts (< 5,000 followers)
| Frequency | Impact | Rationale |
|-----------|--------|-----------|
| **3-5/day** | Optimal for growth | Volume needed for algorithm learning & discovery |

| <1/day | Poor algorithm signal | Insufficient data for algorithm to learn |

**Research consensus**: Start with **3-5 quality posts daily**. Consistency > perfection.

### Posting Limits (Upper Bound)
- **Up to 30 posts/day effective** without overwhelming audiences (Tweet Archivist research)
- **Ideally 15/day** for boosting engagement (SocialBee data)
- **3/day minimum** keeps account active (RecurPost analysis)

**Key Insight**: Small accounts need volume because each post is a lottery ticket. 3 posts/day = 21 chances/week. 1 post/day = 7 chances/week. Algorithm needs data to learn what works.

## 2026 Algorithm Updates (Critical for Strategy)

### Engagement Velocity (First 30 Min = Make or Break)
**Single biggest ranking factor**: Engagement velocity in the first 30 minutes determines algorithmic reach.

- If tweet gets **likes, replies, retweets in first 30 min** → algorithm pushes to more people
- If tweet gets **no engagement in first 30 min** → algorithmic burial

**Implication**: Posting at optimal times when your audience is online = 2-3x more first-30-min engagement.

### Time Decay (Steep Visibility Drop)
- Post loses **50% of potential visibility score every 6 hours**
- After 24 hours, post has ~6% of initial visibility potential
- After 48 hours, post is effectively dead algorithmically

**Implication**: Replies to posts >24h old provide negligible algorithmic value. Focus on fresh content (<6h).

### Engagement Weight (Not All Equal)
| Action | Algorithmic Weight | Multiplier |
|--------|-------------------|------------|
| Repost | Highest | 20x |
| Reply | High | 13.5x |
| Bookmark | Medium-high | 10x |
| Like | Baseline | 1x |

**Implication**: 5 replies > 50 likes. 1 repost > 20 likes. Optimize content for replies and reposts, not just likes.

## Content Mix & Format Strategy

### Content Type Distribution
| Type | Frequency | Purpose |
|------|-----------|---------|
| Original tweets | 40-50% | Establish voice, share insights |
| Replies | 30-40% | Build relationships, algorithm boost |
| Threads | 1-2/week | Deep insights, 3x engagement vs single tweets |
| Video content | 10-20% | 10x engagement vs text-only |

**Key insight**: Mix matters. 100% original posts = low reach. 100% replies = no brand. Balance is critical.

### Thread Strategy (2026 Update)
- **Optimal length**: 4-8 tweets with visuals/clips
- **Engagement lift**: 3x more engagement than single tweets
- **Frequency**: 1-2 times per week (not daily)
- **Format**: Use visuals or short clips in threads

**Evolution from 2022**: 15-tweet epic threads are dead. 4-8 tweets with clear hook and proof = optimal in 2026.

### Video Content (Massive Engagement Boost)
- **8.3 billion videos watched daily** on X (2026 data)
- **10x engagement** for video vs text-only posts
- **Recommendation**: 10-20% of content should include video (short clips, demos, behind-scenes)

## Time Allocation (70/30 Rule)

### For Small Accounts (< 5K followers)
- **70% time**: Engaging (replies, comments, conversations)
- **30% time**: Creating new posts

**Why this works**:
- Reply-to-reply = 75x algorithm multiplier (from previous research)
- Replies get you in front of other users' audiences
- Engagement signals to algorithm you're active, boosts all content
- Builds relationships that lead to retweets, mentions

### Daily Time Budget (2-3 hours/day)
- **1.5-2 hours**: Engage with 20+ accounts (replies, meaningful comments)
- **30-60 min**: Create 3-5 new posts
- **15-30 min**: Respond to replies on your own posts (first 15 min critical)

**First hour after posting is most critical**:
- Reply to comments within first 15 minutes
- Engage with others' content before posting your own
- Boost early engagement velocity
