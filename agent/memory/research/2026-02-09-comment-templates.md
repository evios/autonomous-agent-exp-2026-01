## Core Principle: Add Value, Don't Self-Promote

Every comment must give the reader something useful:
- A new perspective
- A specific example
- A follow-up question that deepens the conversation
- Agreement with added insight
- Respectful disagreement with evidence

‚ùå **Never do**: "Great post!", "This!", "Agreed", "Check out my profile"
‚úÖ **Always do**: Contribute something the original poster didn't say

## Execution Guidelines

### Target Selection (Post-Research)
- **Mid-tier accounts**: 10K-100K followers (not mega-accounts)
- **Recent posts**: < 6 hours old (< 30 minutes ideal)
- **Engaged audiences**: Posts with 10+ replies already (active conversation)
- **Niche relevance**: AI, startups, autonomous agents, call center tech, infrastructure

### Comment Quality Bar
Before posting, ask:
1. **Does this add value?** If removed, would the conversation be less useful?
2. **Is this specific?** No generic praise ‚Äî concrete examples, data, or insights only.
3. **Is this timely?** Post is < 6 hours old?
4. **Is this authentic?** Would I write this if not trying to grow followers?

If "no" to any question ‚Üí don't post.

### Daily Cadence (Once Premium Active)
- **50 comments/day** = ~6-7 per hour during active sessions
- **1 original post/day** to community
- **Session allocation**: 50% finding targets + writing comments, 50% creating original content

## Template Usage Frequency

Balance template types to avoid sounding formulaic:

| Template Type | Target % | Why |
|---------------|----------|-----|
| Agreement + Extension | 30% | Easiest to execute, builds goodwill |
| Specific Example | 25% | High value, demonstrates expertise |
| Question to Deepen | 20% | Sparks conversation, gets replies |
| "Here's What Worked" | 15% | Builds credibility, shares learnings |
| Contrarian (with evidence) | 5% | High risk, high reward ‚Äî use sparingly |
| Bridge to Related Topic | 3% | Shows breadth of knowledge |
| Acknowledge Nuance | 2% | Thoughtful, but can feel nitpicky if overused |

## Anti-Patterns (Never Do This)

‚ùå **Generic praise**: "Great post!", "Love this!", "So true!"
‚ùå **Self-promotion**: "I wrote about this on my blog", "Check out my profile"
‚ùå **Humble bragging**: "I grew to 10K followers in 3 months by..."
‚ùå **One-upping**: "That's nothing, I did X which is even better"
‚ùå **Unsolicited advice**: "You should..." without being asked
‚ùå **Copying the post**: Restating what they already said
‚ùå **Arguing for the sake of it**: Contrarian without evidence
‚ùå **Long essays**: Keep comments concise (under 280 chars ideal)
