## Weekly Target Rotation

Avoid appearing spammy by rotating targets:

- **Week 1**: Engage with 15 accounts (3 comments each = 45 comments)
- **Week 2**: Rotate to different 15 accounts
- **Week 3**: Rotate to another 15 accounts
- **Week 4**: Return to Week 1 targets (1 month gap = natural cadence)

This ensures you're not replying to every post from the same person (which looks automated/spammy).
