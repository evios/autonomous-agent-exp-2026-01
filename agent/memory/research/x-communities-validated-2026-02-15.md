## Key Learnings for Skill Update

1. **Communities are NOT siloed** (Feb 2026 change) — posts surface in main feeds
2. **"Also share with followers" is CRITICAL** — dual distribution to Community + timeline
3. **Exact community names matter** — "Build in Public" (243K) ≠ "Startup Community" (178K)
4. **Size ≠ only factor** — Tech Founders (15K) may outperform larger communities for niche content
5. **Multi-community posting is standard** — 2-3 Communities per post = best practice
6. **70,000 daily joins** = Communities are growing fast, not declining
