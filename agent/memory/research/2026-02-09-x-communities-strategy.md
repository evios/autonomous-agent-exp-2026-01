## Key Finding: Communities Bypass Cold-Start Problem

For accounts under 3,000-5,000 followers, **posting 100% of content to communities** is the recommended strategy to amplify reach beyond your current follower count.

### Why Communities Work for Small Accounts

1. **Immediate Audience Access**: When you post to a community like "Build in Public" (243.5K members), your content appears in a dedicated feed that all community members can access
2. **Algorithm Bypass**: Competition is lower in communities, giving posts a better chance of being seen
3. **Engaged Niche Audience**: Community members are pre-qualified ‚Äî they chose to join based on interest
4. **2026 Visibility Boost**: As of Feb 2026, Community posts are now **visible to everyone** on X (not just members), appearing in main feeds, search results, and recommendations

### Real Results

**Case Study**: One creator gained **almost 2,000 followers in 30 days** by posting 100% of their content into the "Build in Public" community (when it had 30K members, now 243.5K).

**Expected Growth Trajectory**:
- Month 1: 100-300 new followers (posting 5-10x/day to communities)
- Months 2-3: 300-1,000 new followers
