## Recent Tweets (1.5-3 second decision)

**The Scroll Test:** If visitor scrolls your recent tweets, you're 80% to a follow.

**What They're Checking:**
1. **Consistency:** Do you post regularly?
2. **Quality:** Are tweets valuable or noise?
3. **Niche clarity:** Is there a clear theme/expertise?
4. **Engagement:** Are people interacting with you?

**Optimization Strategy:**
- Top 3-5 recent tweets = your "storefront"
- Curate feed by deleting/unliking low-performers if needed (controversial but effective)
- Archive old tweets with Twitter Archive tools (clean slate)
- Ensure recent tweets showcase expertise in your niche

**Red Flags That Kill Conversion:**
- Last tweet from 3 weeks ago (inactive account)
- Generic retweets with no original content
- Random off-topic tweets (no clear niche)
- Zero engagement (no replies, likes, or conversation)
- Low-quality or spam-looking content

**Green Lights That Drive Follow:**
- Recent activity (last 24h)
- Clear expertise in specific domain
- People engaging in replies (social proof)
- Mix of original content + thoughtful engagement
- High quality over high volume

## Conversion Optimization Tactics (2026 Playbook)

### Tactic 1: The 3-Second Audit
**Test your own profile:**
1. Open incognito window
2. Visit your X profile
3. Set timer for 3 seconds
4. What impression did you get?

**If you can't articulate WHO this person is and WHY you'd follow in 3 seconds, your profile needs work.**

### Tactic 2: The Stranger Test
Ask someone unfamiliar with your work:
- "What does this person do?"
- "Why would you follow them?"
- "What value do they provide?"

If answers are vague, rewrite bio + pinned tweet.

### Tactic 3: A/B Test Your Pinned Tweet
- Week 1-2: Pin intro thread
- Week 3-4: Pin best-performing tweet
- Week 5-6: Pin lead magnet
- **Track:** Profile visits → followers (via X analytics)

### Tactic 4: Proof in Banner
Replace generic banner with **specific result:**
- "500K+ calls analyzed"
- "7 years in Voice AI"
- "160 PRs shipped by autonomous agent"
- "2 startups founded, 1 exited"

### Tactic 5: Link Strategy
Don't waste bio link on homepage. Drive traffic with intent:
- Demo signup page
- GitHub repo (autonomous agent)
- LinkedIn (networking for B2B)
- Lead magnet landing page
- Ender Turing case study page

**Track clicks** via UTM parameters or short links (bit.ly).

### Tactic 6: Bio Keyword Optimization
Include searchable terms your target audience uses:
- Voice AI
- Call Center AI
- Speech Analytics
- Autonomous Agents
- Agentic Systems
- Founder
- Infrastructure → AI
- Production AI

**Why:** X search + profile discovery surfaces profiles with relevant keywords.
