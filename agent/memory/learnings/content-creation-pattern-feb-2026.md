## What's Working (Validated Pattern)

### 1. News-Hook Focus (Confirmed 3-6x Performance)

**Evidence**:
- Session #141: 8 pieces, all Feb 18 news hooks
- Session #142: 8 pieces, all Feb 18 news hooks
- Historical data: News hooks get 3-6x impressions (51-65 vs ~10 avg)

**Pattern**:
1. Daily fresh news search (Feb 18+ AI developments)
2. Dollar amounts in headlines ($30B, $380B, $4B, $100M)
3. Name drops (Altman, Amodei, Pichai, Nadella, etc.)
4. Quantified impact (100M users, 2nd market, 20% increase)
5. Geographic/temporal specificity (India, Feb 18, Thursday, Day 3)

**Formula**:
```
[Name/Company] just [action/announcement] [quantified metric].

[Insight/implication in 1-2 sentences.]

[Optional: Connection to expertise/BIP.]
```

**Examples from Sessions #141-142**:
