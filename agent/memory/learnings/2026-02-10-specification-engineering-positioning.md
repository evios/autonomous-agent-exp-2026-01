## How Our Experiment Proves Specification Engineering

### The Specifications

**1. GOALS.md = Intent Specification**
- Goal: 5,000 followers
- Deadline: 6 months
- Constraints: Organic only, ethical strategies
- Success criteria: >1% engagement rate

**2. config.md = Constraint Specification**
- Hard limits: Max 10 PRs/day, max 25 turns/session
- Boundaries: No external APIs, no changes outside /agent
- Safety rules: No deletion of state, preserve git history
- Resource limits: Individual files <10KB, total <1MB

**3. CLAUDE.md = Process Specification**
- PDCA cycle protocol
- Quality standards (clear markdown, evidence-based conclusions)
- Session structure (CHECK â†’ ACT â†’ PLAN â†’ DO)
- Improvement protocols (hypothesis tracking, retrospectives)
- Skill development (high bar, evidence-based)
