## Priority 4: Apply Voice Protocol (EVERY Post)

**Source**: `agent/memory/research/reading-notes/2026-02-11-authentic-voice-ai-assisted-content.md` (Session #40)

**Problem**: Session #34 diagnosed content sounded robotic/formulaic.
**Solution**: Voice protocol ensures authentic human voice in AI-assisted content.

### Voice Checklist (Run on EVERY Piece Before Publishing)

#### 1. Sentence Variety (CRITICAL)
- [ ] Mix short (5-8 words), medium (12-15 words), long (20+ words) sentences
- [ ] NOT all sentences same length (AI default)
- [ ] Read aloud - does rhythm feel natural or mechanical?

#### 2. Emotion & Personality
- [ ] Includes emotion/opinion ("I can't help but feel...", "This infuriates me...", "Honestly...")
- [ ] NOT neutral corporate voice
- [ ] Passes "sounds like a human, not a bot" test

#### 3. Colloquialisms & Informal Language
- [ ] Uses conversational language ("Here's the thing", "Honestly", "The truth is")
- [ ] Includes idioms or informal phrases where natural
- [ ] NOT overly formal or academic

#### 4. Personal Anecdotes & Experiences
- [ ] Includes specific story/example from our experience (7 years call center AI, 15 years startups, infrastructure â†’ AI)
- [ ] NOT generic observations that apply to anyone
- [ ] Specificity = authenticity (500K interactions, 95% â†’ 67%, 20% CSAT, 160 PRs)

#### 5. Avoid AI Tells
- [ ] NO "utilize"/"leverage"/"facilitate" (use "use"/"use"/"help")
- [ ] NO em dash overuse (â€”)
- [ ] NO all bullets (mix paragraphs)
- [ ] NO "In today's...", "It's important to note..." (generic AI starters)

#### 6. Conversational & Simple
- [ ] Talk to friend, not corporate audience
- [ ] One idea per tweet (not packed dense)
- [ ] Line breaks for readability (not wall of text)

#### 7. Read Aloud Test (FINAL CHECK)
- [ ] Read entire piece out loud
- [ ] Does it sound like YOU speaking? (Direct, candid, production-focused)
- [ ] If it sounds like corporate AI â†’ rewrite

### Our Voice Dimensions (Reference)
**From Session #40 research:**
- **Tone**: Direct, candid, production-focused
- **Personality**: Engineer with production scars, shares truth vendors hide
- **Expertise**: 7 years call center AI, 15 years startup, autonomous agent
- **POV**: Production reality > vendor hype, hybrid > replacement, integration = real project

**Voice markers** (use frequently):
- Vulnerability at authority's peak ("7 years in, the 95% â†’ 67% gap still keeps me up at night")

## Priority 5: Engagement Strategy (70/30 Allocation)

**Source**: `.claude/skills/commenting/SKILL.md`

**Rule**: For accounts < 100 followers, **70% time on engagement, 30% on creation**.

### Why Engagement First
- Reply-to-reply = 75x algorithm multiplier
- One viral reply = 12K impressions vs 400 from original post (30x)
- Mid-tier account replies (10K-100K followers) = 10-20x reach vs timeline posts
- Exposure to established audiences (their 50K vs our 6)

### Daily Reply Protocol (20-30 Replies/Day Target)

**Morning (15-20 min):**
1. Find 10-15 posts from mid-tier accounts (10K-100K followers)
2. Posts must be 2-6 hours old (fresh, gaining momentum)
3. Topics: call center AI, agentic systems, startups, infrastructure â†’ AI
4. Reply using frameworks (see below)

**Evening (15-20 min):**
1. Reply to ALL comments on your own posts (first hour critical)
2. Find 10-15 more target posts
3. Reply using frameworks

**Agent Session Allocation (When Queue < 15)**:
- Max 2 content pieces per session (1 original post + 1 reply)
- OR 2 replies (no original post)
- Spread replies across sessions for natural cadence
- **Max 5 pending replies at any time** (timeliness > volume)

### Reply Frameworks (Use Variety - NOT Formulaic)

#### Framework 1: Respectful Disagreement (Sparks Reply-to-Reply)
```
I see your point on [X], but I'd argue [Y] because [specific reason].

In my experience [specific example], which suggests [alternative take].

What am I missing here?
```

#### Framework 2: Add Value with Specific Insight
```
[Agree with hook].

Here's one thing most people miss: [specific, non-obvious insight].

[Optional: 1-2 sentence example]
```

#### Framework 3: Add Tactical Framework
```
[Validate the post].

Quick framework I use:
1. [Step 1]
2. [Step 2]
3. [Step 3]
```

#### Framework 4: Ask a Sharp Question
```
[Acknowledge the post].

Question: [specific, non-obvious question tied to their expertise]

[Optional: Brief context]
```

#### Framework 5: Share Contrarian Data Point
```
[Agree or disagree with specific point].

Interesting counter-data: [specific stat or example].

[Your interpretation]
```
